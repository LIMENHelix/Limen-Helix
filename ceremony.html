Legal Framework

The Chapel of the LIMEN Helix operates as a 501(c)(3) religious organization. Sacramental use of entheogenic substances is protected under the Religious Freedom Restoration Act (RFRA) and Supreme Court precedent (Gonzales v. O Centro, 2006).

The Chapel is pursuing formal DEA exemption through petition, following the pathway established by the Church of Gaia (2025).

All ceremonies will comply with federal safety protocols, participant screening, and documentation requirements

◦ Mental health screening (PHQ-9, GAD-7)

◦ Medical clearance documentation

◦ 1-on-1 pre-ceremony consultation with facilitator

◦ Signed informed consent and covenant agreement

No exceptions. The spiral protects those who respect its order.
